How much does the Setup Fee cost?

The Setup Fee is a one-time charge that covers the cost of:

  • Building your Mobile App.
  • Publishing your Mobile App on the Apple App Store with product images.
  • Publishing your Mobile App on the Google Play Store with product images.
  • Setting up an online dashboard where you can customize your Mobile App and use features like Push Notifications, Geo-fence, User Management, etc.
  • 2 training sessions on how to use your online dashboard for your Mobile App.
  • Creating a flyer for your Mobile App and providing a sign holder to use at your business location.

Because every Mobile App we build is different, the cost of your Setup Fee primarily depends on how complex your Mobile App is. On average, our clients pay $2750 for their Setup Fee. Your Setup Fee may cost more or less depending on the complexity of your Mobile App.

Why does our Ultimate Plan start at $175 / month?

Our Ultimate Plan is a monthly charge that starts at $175 per month to cover the costs of:

  • Continually keeping your Mobile App up-to-date with the latest versions of iPhone and Android.
  • Continually updating your Mobile App with the latest technologies.
  • Maintaining the online dashboard for your Mobile App.
  • Fees associated with using certain features such as Push Notifications, etc.
  • Providing technical support and continual guidance on how to best use your Mobile App to grow your business.

Because every Mobile App we maintain is different, the monthly cost of your Ultimate Plan primarily depends on how complex your Mobile App is. In general, the monthly cost of your Ultimate Plan will be 10% of your Setup Fee with $175 per month being the absolute minimum monthly cost we require. For example, a Mobile App with a $2500 Setup Fee will have an Ultimate Plan priced at $250 per month, and a Mobile App with a $1400 Setup Fee will have an Ultimate Plan priced at $175 per month since $175 is the absolute minimum monthly cost we require. Your Ultimate Plan may cost more or less depending on the complexity of your Mobile App.

What is the total cost of building a Mobile App with us?

On average, our clients pay $2750 for their Setup Fee and $275 per month for their Ultimate Plan. Your total cost for building a Mobile App with us may be more or less depending on the complexity of your Mobile App.

How long will it take to build my Mobile App?

Once we have all the information we need from you in order to begin building your Mobile App, it will take us around three weeks to build and publish your Mobile App. Every Mobile App we build is different, so building your Mobile App may take less time or more time depending on the complexity of your Mobile App.
