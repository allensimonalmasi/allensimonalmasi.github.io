How do your customers see Push Notifications?

Your customers will directly and instantly receive your Push Notification on their mobile phone. Use Push Notifications to directly and instantly deliver any message to your customers. For example, you can send a message to your customers about special deals, new products, announcements, upcoming events, or anything else you'd like!

How can you use Push Notifications to boost sales?

With our platform, you can send Push Notifications to your customers in many different ways. Boost sales by using these different methods to send Push Notifications to specific customers at the right place and the right time.

Groups and Segmentation

By using Groups and Segmentation, you can send Push Notifications to a customer, a specific group of customers, or all customers.

Geo-Fence

By using Geofence, you can schedule Push Notifications to be automatically sent to your customers once they enter a specific geo-location, like near your business location for example.

Scheduled Notifications

By using Scheduled Notifications, you can schedule Push Notifications to be automatically sent on a time-based schedule or under certain conditions like blog updates and etc. Even message customers based off their activity in your Mobile App!

How do you send Push Notifications to customers?

With our platform, sending Push Notifications to customers is incredibly easy. All you need to do is log in with your account on our website, navigate to the Push Notifications section, fill in the form, click on Send Notification and your customers will directly and instantly receive your message on their mobile phone!
